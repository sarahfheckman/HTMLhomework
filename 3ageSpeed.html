Adoption Speed by Age

\

The adoption speed classes are defined as followed:

0 = Pets adopted on the first day their profile was listed 1 = Pets adopted within the first week (1-7 days) of being listed 2 = Pets adopted within the first month (8-30 days) of being listed 3 = Pets adopted within 2-3 months (31-90 days) of being listed 4 = No adoption after 100 days of being listed (no pets were adopted between 90-100 days)
