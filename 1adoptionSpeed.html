Adoption Speed Class

\

The adoption speed classes are defined as followed:

0 = Pets adopted on the first day their profile was listed


1 = Pets adopted within the first week (1-7 days) of being listed


2 = Pets adopted within the first month (8-30 days) of being listed


3 = Pets adopted within 2-3 months (31-90 days) of being listed


4 = No adoption after 100 days of being listed (no pets were adopted between 90-100 days)

Analysis

This shows that while a majority of pets get adopted within 1-3 months of having a profile created, 30% of pets still go unadopted. Where does the cause lie here? There are several alleys to explore within this dataset. Does it have more to do with physical qualities of the pets, such as age or breed, or does it have more to do with the profiles created? A 70% Adoption rate is a passing grade, but nontheless--there is a lot of room for growth.
